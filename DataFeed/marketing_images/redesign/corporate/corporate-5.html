Our Affiliates

Curacao Business Center

Located in the Pico-Union district of Los Angeles, the Curacao Business Center, a pair of towers with ten floors each and half a million square feet of executive office space, houses our corporate offices in the upper levels and our oldest store on the first floor. In addition, several non-profit organizations that provide services to the Latino community make their home there as well.

Curatel

CuraTel is a licensed telephone company providing internet access and local and long distance services. CuraTel's services are often complemented by Pasito.com, enhancing its services with online content and utilities.

Pasito

Established in 2002, Pasito.com began as a dial-up service provider but has now been completely transformed into a full-pledged provider of online content and online services.

Curacao Financial

Established in 2006, Curacao Money Transfer was formed to meet the specific remittance needs of our customers. Our Money Transfer service distinguishes itself by associating exclusively with the most solid and reputable financial institutions in Mexico and Central America. This type of credibility, combined with exceptional customer service, cutting-edge systems and top-notch professionals, makes Curacao Money Transfer a world-class organization regulated by the DFI (Department of Financial Institutions).

Campero

Pollo Campero, a well-known restaurant brand in Central America, offers customers the most mouthwatering chicken and a variety of delicious side dishes. It first opened in Los Angeles 2002 and has now expanded to 17 locations in Southern California and one in Phoenix, Arizona.

Curacao Travel

Curacao Travel is a privately held company based in Los Angeles, CA that is run by seasoned professionals with over 75 years of combined experience in the travel industry. We have extensive experience handling all types of travel situations and customer support issues. our team has previously worked at major airlines, hotel companies and tour operators. Curacao Travel is a fast growing travel agency that has been in service since April 2002. We have the resources and expertise to improve the quality of your vacation experience. Whatever part of our planet you wish to explore, our Vacation Planners can help you make the right choices and guide you to enriching experiences. Our Vacation Planners can assist you with the purchase of airline tickets, hotel stays, all-inclusive vacations, tours, cruises, and more. We negotiate special deals with leading airlines, hotels, resorts and cruises, the brands that you know and trust to offer you amazing bargains.
