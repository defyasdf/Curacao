What is ADIR Global Protection?

Protection for you and your loved ones.

Life is full of unplanned events. With ADIR Global Protection your payments are protected and your credit is secure. With ADIR Global Protection, your payments will be suspended or your debt completely cancelled in case of an unexpected life event. ADIR Global Protection gives you peace of mind knowing that your good name and good credit are protected.
